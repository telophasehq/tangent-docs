---
title: "MSK Source"
description: "Ingest from Amazon MSK (Kafka)."
---

**Config (typical)**

- `type: msk`
- `brokers` (list of `host:port`)
- `topic` (string)
- `group_id` (string)
- `sasl` (optional)
  - `mechanism`: `plain` | `scram-sha-512` | `aws_msk_iam`
  - `username`, `password` (if applicable)
- `tls` (optional)
  - `ca_file`, `verify` (bool)

**Example**

```yaml
sources:
  kafka_in:
    type: msk
    brokers: ["b-1:9092","b-2:9092"]
    topic: logs
    group_id: tangent
````

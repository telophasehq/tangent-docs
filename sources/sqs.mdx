---
title: "SQS Source"
description: "Ingest messages from AWS SQS."
---

Consume messages from **Amazon SQS** and feed them into your plugin.

**Config (typical)**

- `type: sqs`
- `queue_url` (string)
- `region` (string)
- `wait_time_seconds` (number, default: 10)
- `max_messages` (1â€“10, default: 10)
- `visibility_timeout_seconds` (number, optional)

**Example**

```yaml
sources:
  sqs_in:
    type: sqs
    queue_url: https://sqs.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/123456789012/my-queue
    region: us-west-2
    wait_time_seconds: 10
    max_messages: 10
````

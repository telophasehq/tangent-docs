---
title: "S3 Sink"
description: "Write output to an S3 bucket"
---

**Config**
- `type: s3`
- `bucket_name` (string)
- `region` (string, optional)
- `wal_path` (string, default: `/tmp/wal`)
- `max_file_age_seconds` (number, default: 60)
- `encoding` (ndjson|json|avro|parquet; default: ndjson)
- `compression` (none|gzip|zstd; default: zstd level 3)
- `object_max_bytes` (number; default: 134,217,728)
- `in_flight_limit` (number; default: 16)

Example:

```yaml tangent.yaml
sinks:
  s3_staging:
    type: s3
    bucket_name: my-bucket
    region: us-west-2
    encoding: parquet
    compression: gzip
```
